Unique Handmade Jewelry & Accessories

Thank you for visiting Northern Moon Designs! Did you know that the art of jewelry-making is as old as the human race? Isn't it amazing to think that every time we adorn ourselves in this age-old tradition, we are connecting to this rich past?

My name is Rhonda and this is one of the many reasons I love this craft. Based out of Minneapolis, I specialize in making unique, handcrafted jewelry and accessories inspired by history, fashion, and the natural world. Seashells, glass, and semi-precious gemstones are my favorite materials to work with, and I go to great lengths to locate quality supplies from all over the world.

To see what's currently for sale, please visit my Etsy shop. I hope you find something you love there!
